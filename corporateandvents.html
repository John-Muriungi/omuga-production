Stellar Blockchain Network

On Saturday, June 14th, 2025, the energy was electric at the Westerwelle Startup Haus for the Stellar community event. Stellar's mission—to create a decentralized, transparent digital ledger for the world—was palpable in the room. Our photography aimed to reflect this core principle: capturing the clarity of thought during technical talks, the secure connections being forged between developers, and the open exchange of ideas across the network of attendees.

This wasn't just an event; it was a hub for the technology that powers secure, low-cost financial transactions across borders. We focused on authentic moments that tell the story of a community building a more accessible financial infrastructure.

Base: Building the On-Chain Future (August 2025)

The momentum continued on Saturday, August 30th, 2025, with a dynamic meetup for the Base blockchain network. Incubated by Coinbase, Base is designed to be the secure, low-cost, and developer-friendly foundation for the next generation of decentralized applications (dApps).

Our visual production for this event focused on the collaborative spirit and creative energy of the developers and builders in attendance. From deep-dive discussions on mini-apps to starters sharing their first projects, we captured the hands-on, builder-centric culture that Base fosters. The imagery reflects a community not just talking about the on-chain future, but actively coding it into reality.
